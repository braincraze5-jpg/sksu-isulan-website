Admission Guidelines

Freshmen Requirements

  • Original and Photocopy of High School Card (Form 138)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Authenticated Copy of Birth Certificate from PSA
  • Medical Certificate (from University Clinic)
  • 4 pcs. 2x2 ID Pictures with name tag (white background)
  • 1 long brown envelope

Transferee Requirements

  • Official Transcript of Records (TOR) or Copy of Grades
  • Honorable Dismissal
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Authenticated Copy of Birth Certificate from PSA
  • Medical Certificate (from University Clinic)
  • 4 pcs. 2x2 ID Pictures with name tag (white background)

Admission Process

  1. Submit all required documents to the Admissions Office.
  2. Undergo an interview with the college dean or representative.
  3. Take the university entrance examination on the scheduled date.
  4. Receive the admission slip upon passing the exam and interview.
  5. Proceed with the enrollment process.
