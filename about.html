About Our Campus

Our History

Sultan Kudarat State University (SKSU) was established on December 5, 1990, through Republic Act 6973. It was originally founded as Sultan Kudarat Polytechnic State College (SKPSC) by merging five high schools in the province. The Isulan Campus is one of these foundational pillars, evolving into a center for technological and engineering education.

On January 18, 2010, SKPSC was officially converted into Sultan Kudarat State University, marking a new era of academic excellence and commitment to serving the community.

Mission

To provide quality higher education, research, and extension services that are responsive to the needs of the community and the industry.

Vision

A trailblazer in arts, science, and technology in the region.
